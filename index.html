Data Model

All state lives in localStorage under key "genesis-way-v1". The shape below matches what both existing and new screens need.

ModelKey FieldsNotes
DumpItem id, text, bucket, spoke, filter, parked, sourceStage bucket: time | energy | attention | emotional
RhythmAnchor spoke, anchorText, cadence cadence: daily | weekly | monthly
FillAction spoke, action, when, done One per spoke; required before marking Fill complete
ExecutionAssignment spoke, assignment, deadline, firstStep, status status: planned | in-progress | finished
DeadlineMap d30, d90, d180, d365 Each field is a plain string finish-line statement
ParkItem id, text, category, sourceStage, spoke category: week | month | someday

Copy Rules (from Worksheets)

DO use: Ownership language. Clarity, alignment, focus, finishing well. Short declarative sentences. Keep tone calm and practical.

AVOID: Productivity hustle framing. Phrases like "optimize," "hack," "achieve more." Do not imply the user is behind or failing during Dump/Shape screens.

Wheel of Life — Spoke Scoring (Ziglar Model)

The Wheel of Life is an optional diagnostic used at Week 3 (Shape It) and every 6 months. Each spoke is scored 1–10 across 10 sub-dimensions → average = spoke score. Consider building a spoke-score visualization to complement the imbalance indicator on the Shape screen.
