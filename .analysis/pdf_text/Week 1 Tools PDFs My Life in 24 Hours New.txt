My Life in 24 Hours 
Week 1: A Biblical Perspective on Time, Purpose, and Urgency 
Overview 
My Life in 24 Hours helps you see your entire life through the lens of a single day 
so you can steward the time God has entrusted to you with clarity and intention. 
Scripture reminds us: “Teach us to number our days, that we may gain a heart of 
wisdom.” (Psalm 90:12) 
This tool is not about fear or pressure…it’s about perspective, accountability, and 
focus. 
 
Step 1: Understand the Framework 
Assume the average lifespan is 80 years. 
Imagine your entire life lived in one 24-hour day. Assume you sleep 8 hours each 
day… that leaves 16 hours to live. 
• Your life begins at 7:00 AM 
• Your life ends at 11:00 PM 
• Every 12 minutes = 1 year 
• Every 1 minute = 1 month 
• Every 2 hours = 1 decade
