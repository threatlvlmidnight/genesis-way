THE GENESIS WAY – Week 01                 Going Deeper in the Word 
Week One of The Genesis Way confronts the reality that time is not random...it's created by 
God and entrusted to us as a sacred stewardship. Drawing from Genesis 1 and Psalm 90, the 
lesson challenges us to stop drifting through life and begin numbering our days with wisdom and 
intention. When we recognize that our days are limited, clarity replaces chaos and purpose 
replaces pressure. This opening week sets the foundation for the entire series by reframing time 
not as an enemy to manage, but as a gift to steward for God’s glory. 
QUESTIONS  
Read SCRIPTURE: GENESIS 1:1-5 
 
1. WHAT IS ONE WORD THAT BEST DESCRIBES HOW YOUR LIFE FEELS RIGHT NOW—
BUSY, FOCUSED, SCATTERED, PEACEFUL, PRESSURED, OR HOPEFUL? Why that 
word? 
 
2. WHERE DO YOU FEEL THE MOST TENSION IN YOUR USE OF TIME RIGHT 
NOW? Work, family, rest, spiritual life, or expectations from others? 
 
3. IF GOD HAS ALREADY NUMBERED OUR DAYS, THEN DO YOU THINK THAT WASTING 
TIME IS NOT A SCHEDULING ISSUE BUT RATHER A SPIRITUAL ONE. Where does that 
statement feel true…or too strong? 
 
4. WHAT STOOD OUT TO YOU MOST FROM THE TEACHING ABOUT TIME BEING 
CREATED BY GOD, NOT OWNED BY US? (Listen to the C3 Network podcast 
#140) 
 
5. IF YOUR LIFE WERE ONE 24-HOUR DAY, WHAT “TIME OF DAY” DOES IT FEEL 
LIKE FOR YOU RIGHT NOW? HOW DOES THAT AWARENESS CHANGE THE 
WAY YOU THINK ABOUT TODAY? 
 
6. WRITE ONE SENTENCE ANSWERING THIS QUESTION: “BECAUSE THIS IS THE 
SEASON I’M IN, GOD MAY BE INVITING ME TO FOCUS MORE INTENTIONALLY ON 
______.” (If you like, request the “My Life in 24 Hours” PDF from Dan if you want to go 
deeper this week.)
