THE GENESIS WAY – Week 06                 Going Deeper in the Word 
This final week of The Genesis Way focuses on finishing what God has entrusted to you. 
Scripture shows us that faithfulness is measured not by how well we start, but by how well we 
complete the work God assigns. Deadlines are not pressure—they bring clarity, urgency, and 
focus to what matters most. You may not control how much time you have, but you can choose 
to run with purpose and finish well. 
QUESTIONS  
Read SCRIPTURE: GENESIS 2:1-3 
1. DO YOU THINK MOST PEOPLE DON’T FAIL BECAUSE THEY CAN’T START—THEY 
FAIL BECAUSE THEY NEVER DECIDE TO FINISH? 
 
2. WHY DO DEADLINES OFTEN FEEL UNCOMFORTABLE—EVEN WHEN THEY BRING 
CLARITY AND FREEDOM? 
 
3. WHAT’S THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN PLAYING “NOT TO LOSE” AND RUNNING “TO 
WIN” IN EVERYDAY LIFE? (See 1 Corinthians 9:24-27) 
 
4. WHICH AREA OF THE WHEEL OF LIFE MOST NEEDS A CLEAR FINISH LINE RIGHT 
NOW—SPIRITUAL, FAMILY, CAREER, PHYSICAL, MENTAL, SOCIAL, OR FINANCIAL? 
 
5. HOW DOES VIEWING TIME THROUGH THE LENS OF ETERNITY CHANGE HOW YOU 
APPROACH UNFINISHED WORK? 
 
6. WHAT HAS GOD CLARIFIED FOR ME OVER THESE SIX WEEKS? WHAT MUST I 
FINISH NEXT? Consider praying and committing unfinished assignments to God with 
confidence rather than anxiety. 
 
 
NEXT STEPS / CHALLENGE: Consider setting one meaningful deadline in three areas of life: 
One spiritual deadline, one relational deadline, and one work or calling deadline. Each deadline 
should be written down, dated, achievable, and finished…not ongoing…because finishing 
creates momentum. Momentum fuels faithfulness. DEADLINES DON’T LIMIT OBEDIENCE—
THEY ACTIVATE IT.
